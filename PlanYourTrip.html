You can get to The Emerald Island by plane, ferry or you can get a rental car to explore it at your own pace

Getting here

Getting to Ireland by plane is possibly the fastest and easiest way. There are a number of non-stop flights to our most popular Airports: Dublin, Cork, Shannon. Please use the link below to find the best flight for you

Flights to Ireland

You can travel to Ireland using your own car and it might be cheaper than the combination of booking a flight and renting a car. To travel by ferry might take some time, so plan your trip in advance. You can travel form the mainland Europe to Ireland directly via Spain or France (seabridge connection), or from Holland or France to the United Kingdom and from there to Ireland (landbridge connection). Travelling from the UK to Ireland by Ferry is very easy, please use the link below to find an overview of all the ferry routes to Ireland.

Ferries to Ireland

Renting a car is easy and probably the most flexible way to travel through Ireland. When planning a road trip along the Wild Atlantic Way you can drive at your own pace, stop whatever and whenever you want, for as long as you need. Rent your car in advance, using the link below to find the most convenient pick-up location
